# Architecture: Human-Replacement CRM (B2B Focus)

## 1. Vision
An autonomous CRM that manages the entire top-of-funnel and middle-of-funnel autonomously. 
- **Goal:** Minimize human touch until the "Closing" stage.
- **AI Agent:** Acts as a Sales Development Representative (SDR).

## 2. Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Flutter Web (Premium Dashboard)
- **Backend:** Node.js (Express/TypeScript) + Supabase
- **Automation:** n8n + Groq/OpenAI (Intelligent Routing)

## 3. Core Database Schema (Supabase)
### `leads`
- `id`, `name`, `email`, `company`, `linkedin_url`
- `qualification_score` (0-100)
- `status` (New, Qualifying, Nurturing, Ready for Human, Closed)
- `ai_summary` (Generated by IA)

### `interactions`
- `id`, `lead_id`, `channel` (Email, WhatsApp, LinkedIn)
- `content`, `sentiment` (Positive, Neutral, Negative)
- `direction` (Inbound, Outbound)

## 4. Autonomous Workflows
1. **The Qualifier:** When a lead is added, n8n searches for the company details, LinkedIn profile, and scores the lead.
2. **The Nurturer:** AI sends personalized educational content based on the lead's industry.
3. **The Scheduler:** Once qualified, the AI sends a link to book a call with a human closing agent.

## 5. UI Requirements
- Real-time notification of "Ready for Human" leads.
- "Live Chat" where AI handles 90% of queries.
- Analytics on "Human Hours Saved".
